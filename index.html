India

India (official name: Republic of India , English : Republic of India , transliteration : Republic of India ) is the largest country in the Indian subcontinent located in South Asia . India is the seventh largest country in the world geographically , while it is the largest country in the world in terms of population [ 19 ] . India is bordered by Pakistan to the west , Afghanistan to the northwest, China , Nepal and Bhutan to the northeast, Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east . In the Indian Ocean , India shares its maritime border with Maldives to the southwest , Sri Lanka to the south and Indonesia to the southeast . The Himalayan Mountains lie to its north and the Indian Ocean to the south . The Bay of Bengal lies to the southeast and the Arabian Sea to the west.
